Chapter I

About Me

I don't just analyze data. I chase the "why" behind every number, every anomaly, every dashboard that doesn't tell the full story.

Growing up on fairy tales and video games taught me that every world has hidden patterns waiting to be discovered. That curiosity never left me. It evolved into a career built on asking better questions and building better systems to answer them.

With 4+ years across enterprise BI, startup analytics, and modern data stacks, I have built pipelines that process 40M+ records, cut reporting latency by 30%, and turned raw chaos into dashboards that actually make sense.

But here is the thing: I am never satisfied with "it works." I am the person who stays up optimizing a query from minutes to seconds, who builds a RAG chatbot just to make project context instantly accessible, who automates tedious manual workflows because repetition feels like a broken system begging to be fixed.

I believe data should feel human: clear, beautiful, and purposeful. Whether I am modeling dimensions in Snowflake, scraping 80K records with Python, or designing a Power BI dashboard with drill-throughs that make stakeholders say "oh, I get it now" - I am always learning, always building, always curious.

Currently based in Denver, CO. Open to opportunities where data meets impact.
